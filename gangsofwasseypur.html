Gangs of Wasseypur

Gangs of Wasseypur is an epic Indian crime drama directed by Anurag Kashyap. Set in the coal mafia–dominated region of Dhanbad, the film traces generations of gang wars fueled by revenge, politics, caste, and power.

Key Elements of the Film:

  • Setting: The coal belt of Wasseypur and Dhanbad, Jharkhand.
  • Main Characters:
    • Shahid Khan: Rebel who defies Ramadhir Singh.
    • Sardar Khan: Shahid’s son; ruthless gangster seeking revenge.
    • Faizal Khan: Sardar’s son who becomes the final ruler of Wasseypur.
    • Ramadhir Singh: Powerful politician and coal mafia kingpin.
    • Nagma Khatoon: Sardar Khan’s first wife.
    • Sultana Daku: Sardar Khan’s second wife.
    • Mohsina Hamid: Faizal Khan’s lover.
    • Definite Khan: Fearsome and loyal enforcer.
    • Perpendicular: Sarcastic and sharp-tongued henchman.
    • Nasir: Trusted aide of Sardar Khan.
    • JP Singh: Corrupt police officer.
    • Tasher Khan: Ramadhir Singh’s loyal hitman.
    • Pistol Singh: One of Ramadhir’s men.
    • Yadav Ji: Political ally of Ramadhir Singh.
    • Durga: Faizal Khan’s close associate.
    • Tangad: Shooter and member of Faizal’s gang.
