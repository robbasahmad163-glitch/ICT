Mirzapur

Mirzapur is a gritty Indian crime thriller series on Amazon Prime Video about power, politics, and violence in the lawless city of Mirzapur, focusing on mafia don Akhandanand "Kaleen" Tripathi and his rivalry with brothers Guddu and Bablu Pandit. The series explores themes of loyalty, greed, ambition, and betrayal.

Key Elements of the Series:

  • Setting: The fictional, violent underworld of Mirzapur in Uttar Pradesh, India.
  • Main Characters:
    • Kaleen Bhaiya: The powerful mafia boss.
    • Munna Bhaiya: His volatile son.
    • Guddu & Bablu Pandit: Brothers drawn into crime.
