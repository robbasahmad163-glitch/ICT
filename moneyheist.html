Money Heist

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish crime thriller series that follows a mysterious mastermind known as the Professor, who recruits criminals to execute the biggest heists in history at the Royal Mint and the Bank of Spain.

Key Elements of the Series:

  • Setting: Royal Mint of Spain and Bank of Spain, Madrid.
  • Main Characters:
    • The Professor (Sergio Marquina): The mastermind behind the heists.
    • Tokyo (Silene Oliveira): Impulsive narrator and key crew member.
    • Berlin (Andrés de Fonollosa): Charismatic leader inside the Mint.
    • Nairobi (Ágata Jiménez): Expert in forgery and quality control.
    • Rio (Aníbal Cortés): Young hacking specialist.
    • Denver (Ricardo Ramos): Known for his distinctive laugh.
    • Moscow (Agustín Ramos): Miner and Denver’s father.
    • Helsinki: Serbian soldier, loyal enforcer.
    • Oslo: Helsinki’s cousin and fellow soldier.
    • Lisbon (Raquel Murillo): Police inspector turned ally.
    • Stockholm (Mónica Gaztambide): Former hostage turned crew member.
    • Palermo: Architect of the Bank of Spain heist.
    • Bogotá: Metallurgy expert.
    • Alicia Sierra: Ruthless police inspector.
